Questions
Frequently asked
Do I need programming experience?
No. The course is designed for researchers who are not programmers. Module 3 teaches you to generate R and Python code by describing what you need in plain English. If you can write an email, you can use these tools.
Is this just prompt templates I could find online for free?
The prompts are part of it, but the real value is the method ‚Äî how to structure your workflow, when to use which tool, and how to integrate AI into a real research routine. Every prompt was tested on actual papers, grants, and reviewer responses.
Can I use this with sensitive or unpublished data?
Yes. Module 4 teaches you to install completely private AI on your own laptop using Ollama. Your data never leaves your machine ‚Äî no cloud, no API calls, no data sharing. GDPR compliant by design.
My university already offers AI training. How is this different?
Most university AI training covers the basics: "here's ChatGPT, here's what it can do." This goes far deeper ‚Äî practical integration into your academic workflow including grant writing, code generation, literature management, and building your own private knowledge base.
Is AI use ethical in academic research?
Yes, when disclosed properly. Module 8 covers policies from Nature, Science, the Norwegian Research Council, and EU Horizon Europe. The universal rule: use AI freely, disclose it transparently.
Can you run a workshop at our institution?
Yes. I offer half-day and full-day workshops for research groups, departments, and companies. Available in-person across Norway or online worldwide. Email hello@aiforacademics.io for availability.
